Follow My Journey

Elementary School

SD Negeri 251 Jamika

I had a memorable journey at SD Negeri 251 Jamika, where I was honored to be the overall champion (juara umum) and the highest scorer in the national exams. I also represented my school in the Science Olympiad (OSN IPA) and achieved extracurricular success by winning 1st place in the district-level scouting poetry competition for boys and 3rd place in Arabic calligraphy.

Junior High School

SMP Negeri 5 Bandung

At SMP Negeri 5 Bandung, I embraced opportunities to grow both academically and personally. I won 1st place in the "Kim Dengar" scouting competition at the city level and represented my school in the Social Science Olympiad (OSN IPS) at Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia, although the event was canceled due to COVID-19. I also discovered a passion for public speaking by winning 1st place in the school-level Islamic preaching competition.

Senior High School

SMA Negeri 6 Bandung

My time at SMAN 6 Bandung was marked by significant achievements. I ranked 1st parallel in the Mathematics and Science track and was named the best graduate in my class year. I also earned 1st place in the Religious Olympiad at the city level and represented my school in the Mathematics Olympiad (OSN Matematika).

Universty

Institut Teknologi Bandung

Becoming a student at the Bandung Institute of Technology (ITB) is a dream come true and a milestone I have aspired to since childhood. I am proud to be part of this prestigious institution, where I am continually inspired to pursue excellence. At ITB, I chose to focus on Earth sciences through the Faculty of Earth Sciences and Technology. This field reflects my passion for understanding the planet we live on and contributes to solving real-world challenges. Currently, I am working hard to achieve new dreams and aspirations here at ITB. Each day is an opportunity to grow, learn, and push my boundaries as I strive to make the most of this incredible journey. Being here is not just about fulfilling a childhood dream; it is about creating a future filled with purpose and impact
